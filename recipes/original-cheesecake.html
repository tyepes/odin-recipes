Original Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch springform pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar and melted butter until well blended . Press mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese, 1 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla with electric mixer until smooth. Blend in eggs one at a time. Pour over crust.
  4. Bake for 60 minutes, or until filling is set. Allow to cool, then refrigerate until chilled.
  5. In a small bowl, combine sour cream, 1/4 cup sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Spread over top of cheesecake.
  6. Remove the sides of the springform pan. Slice and serve plain or topped with fruit or whipped cream.
