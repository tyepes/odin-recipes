Cranberry bread

This is the recipe to make scrumptious cranberry bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon grated orange zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and baking soda. Stir in orange zest, orange juice, melted butter and egg. Mix until just moistened. Stir in cranberries. Pour into prepared pan.
  3. Bake for 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until the bread springs back when lightly touched. Let stand 10 minutes, then remove from pan. Cool on a wire rack.
